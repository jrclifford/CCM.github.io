Process

How a mediation works

Bill's process is structured for efficiency and thoroughness — designed to surface all key issues and push toward durable resolution.

1
Pre-Mediation Statements

Parties submit and exchange written statements at least one week before the session. Bill reviews all materials in advance.

2
Ex Parte Conferences

Following statement review, Bill conducts confidential telephone conferences with counsel for each party to understand positions and objectives.

3
Mediation Session

A brief joint plenary session is followed by breakout sessions. Parties are guided through the facts and then toward monetary positions and resolution.

4
Persistent Resolution

Bill will not abandon a mediation without using all available tools — including brackets, mediator proposals, and neutral evaluations — to reach settlement.
